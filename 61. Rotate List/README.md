
<h3>Rotate List</h3>
<div class="HTMLContent_html__0OZLp" data-track-load="description_content"><p>Given the <code>head</code> of a linked list, rotate the list to the right by <code>k</code> places.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Example 1:</strong></p>
<img alt="" src="assets/1894926d78974bd0bfb722df4d707d30.jpg" style="width: 450px; height: 191px;"/>
<pre><strong>Input:</strong> head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 2
<strong>Output:</strong> [4,5,1,2,3]
</pre>
<p><strong>Example 2:</strong></p>
<img alt="" src="assets/87167fb2e4fd4eb79eddc55de39f16dc.jpg" style="width: 305px; height: 350px;"/>
<pre><strong>Input:</strong> head = [0,1,2], k = 4
<strong>Output:</strong> [2,0,1]
</pre>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Constraints:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The number of nodes in the list is in the range <code>[0, 500]</code>.</li>
<li><code>-100 &lt;= Node.val &lt;= 100</code></li>
<li><code>0 &lt;= k &lt;= 2 * 10<sup>9</sup></code></li>
</ul>
</div>
